Privacy & Terms

About A Gift of Time

A Gift of Time is a free Islamic prayer clock web application built by Tawfeeq Martin. It provides prayer time awareness, Qibla direction, and fasting progress for Muslim families worldwide.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here is exactly what we collect:

  • Location data: Used only in your browser to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction. Never sent to our servers.
  • Email signups: If you voluntarily sign up for updates, we store your name and email. We will never sell or share this information.
  • Donations: Payments are processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store your payment card details.
  • Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data (page views, device type) to improve the app. No personal information is tracked.
  • Cookies: We use local storage to save your preferences (dial theme, location). No third-party tracking cookies.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone.

Terms of Use

A Gift of Time is provided free of charge, as-is, for personal use. Prayer times are calculated using the Aladhan API and may vary slightly from your local mosque. Always confirm with your local community for precise times.

Donations are voluntary and non-refundable. All contributions go toward hosting, development, and keeping the app free for everyone.
